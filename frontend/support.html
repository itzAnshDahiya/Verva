Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my filter?

Every 12 months or 5,000 hours, whichever comes first. Your VERVA device will show a filter indicator and the app sends an automatic reminder. Replacement filters ship free with our subscription plan.

How noisy is the purifier on max speed?

On maximum turbo speed, the VERVA Pro reaches 56 dB — similar to a calm conversation. On sleep mode it drops to just 28 dB, barely audible even in a quiet bedroom.

Can I control it without the app?

Yes! Touch controls on the device let you change speed, set a timer, and toggle auto mode — no phone needed. Wi-Fi enables remote control, scheduling, and air quality history.

What is your return policy?

We offer an unconditional 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied, we arrange a free pickup and process your refund within 5 business days. No questions asked.

Does the purifier remove pet odour?

Yes — the activated carbon layer in every VERVA filter is specifically rated for pet dander and odour elimination. We recommend the "Pet Home" preset in the app for optimal performance.

What does the warranty cover?

Our 2-year comprehensive warranty covers all manufacturing defects, motor failure, and electronics. In year one we offer a no-questions-asked replacement. Filters are not covered under warranty but are included in subscription plans.

Is installation required?

No installation needed at all. Place the purifier in your room, plug it in, and it's ready to use in under 2 minutes. Wi-Fi pairing with the app takes another 3 minutes.

How do I claim warranty?

Contact us via the form opposite, our chat widget, or email support@VERVA.in. Keep your order number handy. We'll guide you through the process step by step.
