Questions & Answers

Is the Pro suitable for open-plan spaces?

Yes, the VERVA Pro handles spaces up to 80m². For very open layouts above 100m², consider the VERVA Max or using two Pro units.

Does it work without Wi-Fi?

Absolutely. All physical controls and auto-mode work offline. Wi-Fi only unlocks remote control, scheduling, and air quality history in the app.

How long do filters last?

12 months or 5,000 hours of operation. The purifier displays a filter life indicator and the app sends reminders automatically.
