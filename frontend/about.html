Mission

We believe everyone deserves to breathe air that's as clean as it is invisible.

That belief drives every decision — from filtration science to the curve of our product's base. No shortcuts. No greenwashing. Just honest engineering.

🔬

Science First

Every product is validated in an independent laboratory before it reaches your home. HEPA H13 certification is our minimum standard, not our ceiling.

🌿

Planet Responsible

Carbon-neutral operations since 2022. All packaging is recycled and recyclable. Our filters use no plastic in the housing and are sent back for responsible disposal.

✦

Designed to Last

Unlike appliances designed for obsolescence, every VERVA model is built for a minimum 10-year lifespan with modular, replaceable parts.

Our Milestones

Three engineers and a shared frustration with ugly, ineffective air purifiers decide to make something better.
